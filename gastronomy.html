Best Breakfast & Brunch in Gracia Barcelona

Nabucco Tiramisu

Famous for their amazing tiramisu, locals come for great coffee, satisfying breakfast and lunch. We love to chill on the relaxing terrace that sits on one of the city’s most beautiful squares.

Sabio Infante

One of the best spots for brunch in Barcelona with specialty coffee and super cute interior. In the evening, it turns into a bar with craft beers and yummy bar snacks.

Lunch & Dinner Spots in Gracia Barcelona

Casa Palet 1920

Founded in 1920s, this delicatessen turned to restaurant is now run by 3rd generation owner Àlex Palet, committed to source best seasonal local produce. Try the Russian salad or fried eggs with jamón and ask for a wine recommendation. You won’t be disappointed.

The Fish & Chips Shop

“It’s time to fuck diet and eat fish and chips!” Fresh, beer-battered, deep-fried fish that melts in your mouth. Thick-cut chips with a spicy twist. Served with homemade tartar sauce and mango chutney. Expect generous portions, good vibes and super friendly service.

Tio Joe Burger House

Enter the spacious and rustic house of Tio Joe aka your Spanish Uncle Joe. Ask him for the juiciest burger with 48hr-marinated prime beef, the crunchy chicken or the wild boar ribs. Seasonal dishes, vegan and halal options. All desserts are homemade.

Restaurant Xavier Pellicer

Michelin-star chef Xavier Pellicer opens doors to his beautiful new home. Combining fine dining technique with his philosophy of eating biodynamic, seasonal and local. Dishes are exquisite and full of surprises. We love the chic yet comfortable space.

Best Tapas in Gracia Barcelona

Bobby Gin

One of the best cocktail bars in town also serves up delicious fusion tapas. Try crispy Vietnamese shrimp tempura or gourmet patata bravas along with gin cocktails made by the award-winning mixologist, Alberto Pizzaro.

El Bandarra Taperia

From chicken croquette to mushroom and butifarra gyoza, Chef Guizy combines traditional Catalan flavors with oriental touches. We love sitting at the terrace onto our favorite Gràcia church plaza.

Entre Hores

This small neighborhood joint creates a uniquely intimate atmosphere, this coziness of being at your friend’s home. They offer tasty tapas, local craft beers and great cocktails.
