Upcoming Events

Festa Major de Gràcia

Aug 15-21, 2019

The main event at Gràcia’s extravagant Festa Major is its street competition, where residents transform some 25 streets into pirate ships, rainforests and Jurassic landscapes.

Where: all over Gràcia

Sa Pobla a Gràcia

Jan 26, 2019 (2020 dates TBA)

Celebration in honour of St Anthony. Two days of Balearic folk festivities see street bonfires, parades of dragons and giants and human towers in the squares..

Where: Gràcia, around Plaça del Diamant, Plaça de la Vila de Gràcia

LEM Festival

When: Sep 27 - Oct 27 (2019 dates TBA)

A month-long, well-organised festival of multimedia art and experimental music. It mostly covers electronica, but also includes jazz and rock. Concerts are generally free.

Where: various venues, Gràcia
